About Us

At Goldenbridge Co., we specialize in transforming homes and workspaces with expert craftsmanship. Our services include Venetian plaster, accent walls, roofing, flooring, and bed bug clearance, ensuring comfort, style, and functionality. From minor upgrades to full renovations, our skilled builders, carpenters, and decorators bring your vision to life—without labor management worries, hidden costs, or delays. Beyond construction, we offer eco-friendly pest control to eliminate bed bugs and professional oven cleaning for a spotless kitchen. Our three-step approach guarantees lasting results for a cleaner, healthier home. Whether you seek a modern makeover or a classic restoration, we deliver top-quality craftsmanship to create elegant, functional spaces. Home remodeling has become increasingly important as it not only enhances the overall appearance of your home but can also save you money in the long run. Rather than the significant investment required to build a new home, home renovation services offer an affordable and effective way to refresh and upgrade your living space. Whether you're looking to update a single room or renovate your entire home, remodeling is an ideal solution for anyone wanting a fresh change and to increase their home's value and functionality. At Goldenbridge Construction, we are a certified, Dublin-licensed home remodeling contractor with years of experience specializing in home renovations across the Dublin area. We understand that every home is unique, and we take the time to listen to your needs and vision before we begin any project. Our team prioritizes customer satisfaction, ensuring that every project is completed to the highest standards with a focus on detail and quality craftsmanship. We pride ourselves on treating each client's home with the utmost respect, as if it were our own. Our philosophy is simple – to make lives better, starting at home. We strive to create living spaces that are not only beautiful but also functional, comfortable, and tailored to your specific lifestyle. Whether you’re considering a kitchen upgrade, a bathroom makeover, or a complete home transformation, Goldenbridge Construction is here to bring your vision to life. Let us help you make your dream home a reality. Contact us today!
